title: data store
level: basic

Data stores refer to places to store data.

## common types and popular examples of data stores
* [databases](/database): persistent storage for data
    * [mysql](/mysql)
    * [postgres](/postgres)
    * [mongodb](/mongodb)
    * [cassandra](/cassandra)
* [cache](/cache): temporary but fast storage of data
    * [memcached](/memcached)
    * [redis](/redis)
* vendor storage: storage managed by a business
    * [aws s3](/s3)
    * [firebase](/firebase)
